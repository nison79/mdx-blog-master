One of the many aspects of human nature that still surprise me for the good, is the ability to transform. You can take a picture of a face beautiful or not looking at you, and the moment when the shutter closes the door the rest of this moment is history and you captivate the beauty or not of that person. 

Human portraits are one of most adored themes for photographers around the world and everyone of them approaches this with a unique way and that is the charm of it.

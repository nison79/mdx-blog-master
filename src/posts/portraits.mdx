One of the many aspects of human nature that still surprise me for the good, is the ability to transform.  You can take a picture of a face beautiful or not looking at you,  and the moment when the shutter closes the door the rest of this moment is history and you captivate the beauty or not of that person. 

Human portraits are one of most adored themes for photographers around the world and everyone of them, approaches this with a unique way and that is the charm of it.

![stella georgiadou](../images/stella-close.jpg)

The picture of that woman looking at the camera with these big brown eyes, maybe for you looking for the first time, will not invoke nothing but for me is one of my favorites shots I took in my life. And let me explain. 

This is my wife, my woman, the mother of my daughter and only this fact makes me let say this. Also, that was a moment so _*casual_, so* pure with no directions or fiction that makes me very happy from the result we face. I managed to capture what i love about her with no effort at all. It was something that just happened. Her's big expressive eyes, the big smile, the angle of her face, the way she looked in the camera, with a glimpse of happiness.
